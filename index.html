About Me

I am a PhD research scholar working in microfluidics, biofluid mechanics, and microswimmer transport physics. My research focuses on sperm rheotaxis and the development of biomimetic microfluidic devices for reproductive health applications. My work integrates experimental microfluidics, high-speed imaging, and computational fluid dynamics to understand microscale cell–fluid interactions.
