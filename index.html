About Me

I am a PhD research scholar working in the field of microfluidics, biofluid mechanics, and microswimmer transport physics. My research focuses on understanding sperm cell rheotaxis in complex physiological fluids and developing biomimetic microfluidic devices for reproductive health and cell separation applications.

My work combines experimental microfluidics, high-speed imaging, and computational fluid dynamics (ANSYS Fluent and COMSOL) to study fluid–structure interactions at microscale.
